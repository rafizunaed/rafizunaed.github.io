Biography

Mohammad Zunaed is currently working as a research assistant in the mHealth Laboratory, Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), where he is advised by Associate Prof. Dr. Taufiq Hasan. His ongoing research involves the application of deep-learning on medical imaging problems (domain generalization, integration of anatomical knowledge). He has completed his B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) from the BUET in 2019. He is now pursuing M.Sc. in EEE from the BUET under the supervision of Associate Prof. Dr. Taufiq Hasan and Prof. Dr. Md. Aynal Haque.
